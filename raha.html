About Our Business

Located in the heart of Kaduna, RAHA Computer Business Center & Mini Café has been serving the community with top-notch digital services and printing solutions since 2015.

We combine technical expertise with a welcoming atmosphere, offering both professional services and a comfortable space to enjoy refreshments while you wait.

Our commitment to quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.

Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a wide range of digital and printing services to meet all your business and personal needs.

Online Registration

Professional assistance with all types of online registrations including JAMB, WAEC, and government services.

Typesetting

Expert document formatting and typesetting for reports, theses, proposals, and other professional documents.

Photocopy Services

High-quality photocopying in various sizes and paper types, including color copying options.

Plastic ID Cards

Custom-designed plastic ID cards for schools, businesses, and organizations with various security features.

Lamination

Document lamination services in various sizes to protect and preserve your important papers and cards.

Binding

Professional binding services including spiral, thermal, and hardcover binding for reports and projects.

Calendar Printing

Custom calendar printing with your photos or designs, available in various sizes and formats.

Sticker Production

High-quality sticker printing for branding, promotions, or personal use with various material options.

Graphics Design

Professional graphic design services for logos, flyers, banners, and other marketing materials.

Car Branding

Complete vehicle branding solutions including design, printing, and installation of car wraps and decals.

Passport Photos

Professional passport photo services meeting all official requirements for various applications.

Mini Café

Refresh yourself with our selection of hot and cold beverages while waiting for your services.
